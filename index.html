the four sides

Pick your corner

01

Red

Red players usually play fast and fearless. You are often in the action early, so every move feels bold.

02

Green

Green is calm and patient. Let others clash first, then slip through when the board opens up.

03

Yellow

Yellow loves momentum. One token can sprint across the board and put everyone else under pressure.

04

Blue

Blue is for careful players. You hold strong positions, wait for mistakes, and punish risky moves.

the basics

How we play

01

The Six

You usually need a 6 to bring a token out of base, and rolling a 6 gives you another chance. Lucky, but dangerous.

02

The Track

Move clockwise and think ahead. Sometimes the safe move is smarter than the flashy one.

03

The Capture

Land exactly on an opponent and they go back to base. That one move can flip the whole match.

04

Home

Get all four tokens home with exact rolls to win. Easy to say, stressful to do.
